InterMine Mobile

InterMine iOS and Android apps#

InterMine smartphone applications allow you to carry the power of multiple InterMines in your pocket. They include a cross-InterMine search tool, allowing you to browse lists, templates, and create a selection of "favourites" - perhaps a set of publications to review or a collection of interesting genes.
